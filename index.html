SBT Architects is an established award-winning design studio based in East London, South Africa, founded in 2010.

The studio has expertise in a range of sectors including residential, commercial, hospitality and mixed-use projects, delivering design servicesto a range of projects across South Africa and the rest of Africa. Our ethos is to provide an intergrated and cohesive approach to architecture in context witht the environment.

SBT Architects provides an in-house construction team which allows clients to leverage on our expertise, ensuring that quality and standards are maintained through in-house collaboration.
